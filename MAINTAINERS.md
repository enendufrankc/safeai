## Expectations

Maintainers are expected to:

- Keep ownership areas and renewal dates current in this file.

- Maintainer status is reviewed every 12 months.
- Renewal is based on sustained review and stewardship activity.
- Maintainers inactive for 90+ days may be moved to inactive status.
